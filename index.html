Hi! My name is Celine Jugandi Pasutti and I am a 15 year old student in Grade 10 attending Queen Elizabeth Highschool in Edmonton, Alberta. Some of my interests are:

  • ➼ Gardening
  • ➼ Skiing
  • ➼ Video Games
  • ➼ Reading/Writing
  • ➼ Coding and Computer Science
  • ➼ Space and Astronomy/Astrology
  • ➼ Makeup and Cosmetics
  • ➼ Art and Music
  • ➼ and anything with caffeine in it

Overall, I get nervous very easily and to put it bluntly I constantly procrastinate- in fact, a running joke in my family is that I procrastinated being born. My mom's due date was December 26, 2005 and I was born January 9, 2006. This level of procrastination unfortunately cost me a year of schooling (but at least I can drive before everyone my grade, right?). Besides procrastination, though, I am pretty responsible with my schoolwork and personal interests. I don't really know how to describe my personality, but typically, my friends describe me as someone who is extremely sarcastic, and doesn't deal with nonsense. Even though I tend to come off a little rough at first, I will always stand up for others and what I believe in with no shame. I don't really care what my peers think of me - I used to get bullied all the time because I wore really weird clothes and acted a little differently than others. I don't really know what happened, but it just occoured to me one day that the opinions of others doesn't really matter and I should stop breaking my back just for the ideals of people I don't even know or like. Besides, I like really weird and that's all that really matters at the end of the end of the day. Socially, I'm kind of dense. I don't pick up on hints or body language very well and unless someone tells me I'm doing something wrong bluntly and straight to the point, I will probably not catch on until i'm taking a shower or laying in bed like two years later. I have really terrible memory too so I forget to do a lot of the stuff my parents ask me to. They always blame this on my phone, but I think this trait is normal for anyone, since most of the people I know are constantly thinking of a million things at once like I am.

Life moves pretty fast. If you don't stop and look around once in awhile, you could miss it.
